Who are we?

Since 2004, the award-winning Taupo Adventure Limited has provided adrenaline filled activities around beautiful Lake Taupo ranging from 15000ft skydives to trips to the geothermal hot pools, Taupo Adventure Limited prides itself on entertaining tourists both domestically and internationally while maintaining their safety as much as possible.

We deliver these six activities:


  • Skydiving
  • Geothermal Pools
  • Hiking
  • Fishing
  • Kayaking
  • Biking

Jason

Born and bred in Taupo, Jason has years of experience in skydiving, biking, hiking and fishing as well as providing excellent customer service. Jason is also a four-time winner of the UCI Mountain Bike Championship and three-time winner of the tour de France.
